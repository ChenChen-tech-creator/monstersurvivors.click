How to Play

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where your goal is to stay alive as long as possible while facing waves of increasingly difficult monsters.

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Collect experience points to level up and unlock powerful abilities. Choose your upgrades wisely to create the ultimate monster-slaying build!

Game Features

  • Endless waves of unique monsters
  • Multiple character classes with different abilities
  • Dozens of upgrades and power-ups
  • Procedurally generated environments
  • Challenging boss fights
  • Leaderboards to compete with friends

Tips & Strategies

Focus on movement speed early to avoid getting surrounded.

Balance offensive and defensive upgrades for the best survival chances.

Don't ignore health regeneration abilities - they're crucial for long runs.

Area-of-effect attacks are essential when monsters start to swarm.

Watch for special item drops from elite monsters!

About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is a thrilling roguelike survival game inspired by classic arcade action. Developed by a passionate team of indie game creators, it combines simple controls with deep strategic gameplay.

The game features beautiful pixel art graphics, an immersive soundtrack, and addictive gameplay that will keep you coming back for "just one more run." With procedurally generated levels and a wide variety of enemies, no two games are ever the same.

Whether you have just a few minutes or want to dive in for hours, Monster Survivors offers accessible yet challenging gameplay for all types of players. How long can you survive?
